This is an android app to browse and favorie dog images from a specific API/Link.
The target users are people interested in images, like pinterest, but specifically for dogs. That's all they want.
They'll have a button to get a feed for a specific breed, chosen through a drop-down menu.
The app shouldn't require sign-in or account, it'll just keep data stored locally. The user will be able to browse a feed of pictures, and they can choose to favorite them to look at later.
